# Customer Experience Team — Context

## Team
120 CS agents across 3 cities + 1 CX Head (Divya Krishnamurthy). Handle inbound tickets via chat, email, and phone.

## Top Ticket Categories (last 30 days, 2.1M tickets total)
1. **Wrong / missing items** (38%) — customer received substitute or item not delivered
2. **Late delivery** (24%) — ETA shown was 10 min, actual was 25–40 min
3. **Payment failure** (16%) — charge deducted but order not placed; UPI timeout issues
4. **Refund not received** (13%) — approved refund pending >48h
5. **App crash / bug** (9%) — checkout crashes on older Android devices

## Recurring Complaints (verbatim from customers)
- "You showed 10 mins but my order came 35 mins late with no updates"
- "The app froze when I hit 'Pay' and money was deducted twice"
- "I ordered 6 items and only 4 came — refund still pending after 3 days"
- "Search doesn't understand Hindi — I typed 'दूध' and got random results"
- "No one told me my order was running late. I had to call to find out"

## SLA Performance
- First response time target: 2 min | Actual: 5.4 min
- Resolution time target: 4h | Actual: 11.2h
- CSAT score: 3.6 / 5

## Feature Requests from CS Team
- Automated refund processing for orders <₹500 (remove manual agent step)
- Proactive WhatsApp/SMS alert when ETA changes by >5 min
- Better order tracking for agents to see real-time dark store status
- Bulk action tools for handling surge periods (bulk refund approval)

## Volume Trend
Ticket volume up 55% MoM as order volume grows. CS headcount cannot scale at same pace — automation is critical.
