### Requirements
* Simplify the checkout flow to reduce drop-offs
* Provide proactive ETA alerts to improve customer experience
* Implement automated refunds for small orders
* Display real-time packing status to reduce support tickets
### Scope
* In: Simplifying the checkout flow, providing proactive ETA alerts, implementing automated refunds, displaying real-time packing status
* Out: Redesigning the entire app, implementing a new payment gateway
### Final Specification
* Simplify the checkout flow: Reduce the number of steps from 5 to 2, and ensure that the flow is intuitive and easy to use
* Provide proactive ETA alerts: Send alerts to customers when the ETA changes by more than 5 minutes, and ensure that the alerts are timely and accurate
* Implement automated refunds: Automate the refund process for orders less than ₹500, and ensure that the process is fast and efficient
* Display real-time packing status: Display the real-time packing status to customers, and ensure that the status is updated in real-time
