1. **Improving Order Completion Rate (OCR)**: The current OCR of 84% is below the target of 90%. To achieve this, we need to focus on reducing drop-offs during the checkout process, improving payment failure rates, and enhancing the overall user experience. This can be achieved through initiatives such as checkout flow redesign, automated refunds, and real-time packing status.
2. **Enhancing Search Relevance**: The poor search relevance for regional language queries is a major pain point, with 21% of searches returning 0 results. To address this, we need to focus on improving search algorithms, incorporating transliteration and regional synonyms, and enhancing the overall search experience.
3. **Reducing Refund Resolution Time**: The current refund resolution time of 48 hours is above the target of 6 hours. To achieve this, we need to focus on automating refund processes, improving communication with customers, and streamlining the refund workflow.
4. **Improving Customer Support**: The high volume of customer support tickets, particularly for wrong/missing items, late delivery, and payment failures, is a major concern. To address this, we need to focus on enhancing the overall customer support experience, providing proactive alerts and updates, and empowering customer support agents with better tools and information.
5. **Increasing Contribution Margin**: The current contribution margin is below the target of 6%, and we need to focus on reducing subsidy burn, improving payment failure rates, and enhancing the overall revenue stream.
